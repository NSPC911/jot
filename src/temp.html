GP allows one to - Undestand the world and themselves through key current issues of local, regional and global significance - Construct cogent arguments to communicate their ideas clearly

Assessment
2 papers in total - Paper 1: Essay - Answer ONE out of a total 8 question shit is crazy - Paper 2: Comprehension

Both is 1h30mins

Content
Questions are centred on - Issues drawn from across disciplines - Issues of local, regional and global concern

Issues and knowledge related to: - Society and Culture - Economics - Politics - The arts and humanities - Science and Technology - The Environment

Note that practically anything under the sun can be tested

GP vs O’ Level English | General Paper | O’ Level English | |——————|——————–| | Only 1 section | 3 sections |

In a nutshell, GP is - Skills - Close reading - Inference - Critical thinking - Logical reasoning - Engaging and convincing writing - Application to real-world context - Knowledge - General knowledge of local, regional and global current affairs - Key themes and issues which will be covered in the next two years
